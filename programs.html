What We Offer

Our dynamic CBC curriculum blends traditional and innovative learning methods to prepare students for a global society, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and real-world skills.

Digital Literacy

Enhances reading, writing, and communication skills through interactive activities.

Education

Focuses on hands-on problem-solving and numeracy skills using real-life examples.

Teenage Mothers Empowerment

Develops communication skills in Kiswahili through interactive learning and storytelling.

Environmental Conservation

Teaches students about nature, sustainability, and environmental conservation.

Welcome to Little Lions Junior School, where we nurture future leaders through education, creativity, and compassion in a collaborative community.
